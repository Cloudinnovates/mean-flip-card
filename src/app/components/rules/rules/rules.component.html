Single Player Rules:
  • Player must select a username and a difficulty
  • Player clicks start button to start the game
  • Player can select 2 cards per turn to attempt a match.
  • If player does not match the card during their turn, they lose a 'miss'
  • Once all misses are used up, the player will lose
  • Each turn, player has an amount of time they must make their move
  • If player does not make their selection during the alloted time, they will lose
  • If player makes all matches before using their misses, they win the game
Multiplayer Rules:
  • Player must select a username, multiplayer game type, and a difficulty
  • Once player starts the game, they will be moved to a lobby until they are matched with another player
  • Once player has been matched to another player, they will be moved to the game room where the game countdown will start
  • The player who was first inside the lobby will start once the countdown ends
  • Players will take turns, trying to match all the cards on the board
  • Once all the cards have been matched, players with the most matched cards wins
  • If player runs out of time during their turn, they will automatically lose
  • If players run out of remaining misses, the player with the most matches will win
  • If a player quits the game, the other player will automatically win and will be redirected back to the page
  • Once the game is over, players can restart. This time, the opposite player will start first
